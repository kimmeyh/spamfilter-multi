Add a new email account. For Gmail, the recommended method is an App Password (IMAP) with 2-Step Verification enabled; Google Sign-In (OAuth) is available as an alternative but may require more frequent re-authentication. For AOL, Yahoo, and other IMAP providers, enter your email and an app password from the provider's security settings.

The "Test Connection" button verifies credentials without saving. If you see "Too many failed sign-in attempts", wait for the displayed unlock time before retrying (sign-in is rate-limited per account to resist brute force).
